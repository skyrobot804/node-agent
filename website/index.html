How it works

You donate the night.
We do the science.

No expertise required. No manual intervention. Your telescope works while you sleep.

01 /

Donate your Seestar

Install the node agent on any computer near your telescope. It joins the network and accepts assignments automatically overnight.

02 /

The scheduler decides

Our engine assigns scientifically valuable targets — supernovae, novae, variable stars — matched to your sky, your hardware, and the network's coverage gaps.

03 /

Your name on real science

Calibrated photometry is submitted to AAVSO under your observer code. You are credited on every accepted observation — permanently, in the scientific record.
